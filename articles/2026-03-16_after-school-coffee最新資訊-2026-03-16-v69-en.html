Quality Standards: The 80-Point Threshold

At the heart of specialty coffee lies a commitment to exceptional quality. The Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) has established the 80-point scoring system as the global benchmark for premium coffee. Any coffee scoring 80 points or above earns the prestigious "specialty grade" designation, representing beans grown in optimal conditions with careful processing and expert roasting.

At After School Coffee, we uphold these rigorous standards. Every batch we source is evaluated using SCA protocols, ensuring that your cup meets the distinction that true coffee enthusiasts expect. This dedication to quality means our customers can trust that every serving meets internationally recognized excellence criteria.

Global Coffee Market Insights

The International Coffee Organization (ICO) continues to monitor global coffee production and consumption patterns that affect our local market. According to their latest data, global coffee consumption has shown steady growth, with specialty coffee commanding an increasingly significant share of the market.

For Macau's discerning coffee drinkers, this translates to greater variety and higher quality expectations. As international demand for premium beans increases, sourcing becomes more competitive. At After School Coffee, we navigate these market dynamics to secure exceptional lots while maintaining accessible pricing for our valued customers.

Climate Resilience in Coffee Production

Climate change poses significant challenges to coffee cultivation worldwide. The World Coffee Research (WCR) has been pivotal in developing climate-resilient coffee varieties that can withstand changing environmental conditions while maintaining flavor quality.

We're proud to partner with farms that prioritize sustainability and innovation. By supporting producers who invest in climate-adaptive agriculture, After School Coffee contributes to the long-term viability of specialty coffee production while delivering consistent, exceptional cups to our patrons.

Expertise and Certification

Quality coffee requires expertise at every stage—from farm to cup. The Coffee Quality Institute (CQI) trains and certifies Q Graders, the industry's most respected coffee tasters. These professionals undergo rigorous testing to ensure accurate flavor assessment and quality verification.

Our team stays current with industry developments, and we continuously invest in education and training. By understanding the Q Grader methodology and cupping protocols, we ensure that every coffee we serve meets the highest standards of excellence.
